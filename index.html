How to Play

Guess the hidden word by choosing letters one at a time. You can earn hints if you need help!

Features

  • Multiple Difficulty Levels: Choose from Easy, Hard, and Master levels.
  • Hint System: Use hints to reveal letters when you're stuck.
  • Progress Tracker: See how well you're doing as you play.
